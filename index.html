Technology should help us, but many apps are made to keep us hooked. Use this guide to understand how your phone affects you and find simple ways to build better habits by the renewal of our minds. To move from a culture of digital secrecy to one of authentic presence, we require a structured, church-led intervention. This is the heart of the "Church Without Curtains" movement: pulling back the veil of digital dependency to reveal the person God intended us to be
